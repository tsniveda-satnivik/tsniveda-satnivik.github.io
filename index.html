What We Offer

Four ways we
can help you.

From a one-time audit to ongoing fractional advisory — we meet you where you are. Every engagement starts with understanding your context, not selling you a package.

01 🔍
Support Infrastructure Audit
A deep, structured review of your entire support setup — tools, workflows, ticket handling, response times, escalation paths, and team coverage. Delivered as a clean PDF report with a readout call.
  • Ticket flow & volume analysis
  • Top 5 gap identification
  • Immediate quick wins document
  • 30/60/90 day improvement roadmap
  • 1-hour readout call with Q&A
02 ⚙️
Implementation & Tooling Setup
We don't just recommend tools — we set them up. From Zendesk and Freshdesk to Intercom and open-source alternatives, we configure your helpdesk to match your actual workflow, not a default template.
  • Platform selection & setup
  • Ticket routing & automation rules
  • Canned responses & macros
  • Integration with existing tools
  • Agent onboarding & training
03 📋
Process Design & SOPs
Support without documentation is organised chaos. We create the runbooks, escalation frameworks, response templates, and SOPs your team needs to stay consistent at any volume.
  • Escalation path design
  • Response SOP library
  • Runbooks for common issues
  • Knowledge base structure
  • Handoff & shift coverage docs
04 🤝
Fractional Support Advisory
Not ready for a full-time support lead? We act as your fractional Head of Support — setting strategy, overseeing your team, and optimising operations as you scale. Priced to match your actual usage.
  • One free discovery audit call
  • One-time onboarding fee
  • Per-ticket / per-chat billing based on load
  • Monthly performance reviews
  • Scales up or down with your volume
